The purpose of stretching is to lengthen your muscle fibers through either static or dynamic movements. Doing this can help to minimize injury when engaging in any other form of exercise, however you have to be careful not to injure yourself when you stretch. There are many forms of stretches, each targeting a specific muscles or muscle group. Yoga is one of the most popular disciplines that involves stretching.
